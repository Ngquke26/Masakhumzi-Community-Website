ABOUT US

This is a community page that is dedicated to fixing and developing the Masakhumzi Community and helping our community members to develop skills in any of their fields of interest.

MISSION

To provide a safe and secure environment for all residents of the Masakhumzi Community .Our goals is to transform our community into a productive, educative, healthy, and environmentally safe society, ensuring that our residents have easy access to everything they need to thrive.

VISION

Our vision is to become the number one community in the province that prioritizes its residents by ensuring that everyone has access to all basic and secondary services needed for a good quality of life.
