Assistance Teachers

The minimum requirements for this position is Grade 12.The most people we will be taking is the youth to eliminate crime, the intake of drugs amd upskill them.Their duty is to assist teachers with the amount of work they have by monitoring revision for the learners and helping them with understanding in the fields they struggle the most in.


Social work

>Parents will be employed to work with qualified social workers in schools to deal with personal issues of student ,offer advices and emotional support to students who have family issues and academic struggles.

Care givers

A group of adults will be employed from our community to become caregivers in clinics,home centres and individual houses to assist adults who cannot takecare of themselves and the one who have no family members ,they must be prepared to live with them on their homesteads ,home centres.


Construction

We will be strictly hiring females and males who are physical healthy between ages of 25-40 years,they will be distributed to different regions.Every member of the community is more than welcome to apply.Duties involve road,bridges and buildings construction,developing transportation systems,establishing urban planning and development strategies,management of construction projects and conducting infrastructure assessments and mantainance.Suitable candidates will be unemployed unskilled youth,unemployed qualified civil engineers,urban planners and architects for building clinics,schools and libraries.No experience is required.


Feeding schems

We need helpers who are between 35-49 years who will be cooking in schools with the goal of helping children who are from disadvantaged families to get something to eat before their classes and on lunch times so that they can be able to focus on their studies because we believe that there is no learner who can participate and be active in a class on an empty stomach.This is also to help parents to get some income so that they can take care of themselves and their children.


Waste Managemnet

This involves collection ,transportation and disposal of waste,developing waste reduction and recyling strategies,operating landfills,incinerators and recycling facilities,and implementing waste-to-energy solutions.Unemployed qualified professionals like environmental engineers ,waste management specialists and public health specialists and also unskilled people are welcomed to apply for this project as there will be training involved to upskill people .


Inter houses

Work involves coaching,or training activities,managing local sport teams and events.Our goal is to help teenagers so that thye can have something to do in the afternoons after school to stop them from focusing on drugs and alcohol.We will be hiring the youth to be the ones who manages these activities as it will help in eliminating crime on the community and for them to have bigger dreams as they will be getting paid.


Security forums

This department involves creating community forums where the community members will be hired to clean the community from drugs and holding anyone who is selling drugs accountable.Patrolling activities will be held at night to protect every community household from criminals,making sure that there is no one who is roaming on the streets at night with dangerous weapons.We will be strictly respected male members of the community who are between 35-60 years.This to also make sure that parents can provide for their families and can protect our community accordingly.No experience is required but community leaders will have a say on who is hired.


Farming

This department involves crops and livestock production,agricultural research and development .Farm management and operations ,agricultural extention services,food processing and safety.Every member of the community is more than welcome to applyDuties involve road,bridges and buildings construction,developing transportation systems,establishing urban planning and development strategies,management of construction projects and conducting infrastructure assessments and mantainance.Suitable candidates will be unemployed unskilled youth,unemployed qualified civil engineers,urban planners and architects for building clinics,schools and libraries.


Sewing Activities

Work involves designing and creating clothing textiles like school,nursing and construction uniform,altering and repairing existing garmets.Teaching sewing techniques,developing and marketing these product.We will be taking the youth and adults,no experience is required.


Every individual who will be participating on the activities will get a stipend and some will get permanent employement at the end of the contracts which will depend on the work ethic and behavior because the municipality is trying to eliminate poverty and crime and for people to have better buying power.
